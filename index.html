Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving technological advancements, fostering entrepreneurship, and embracing digital solutions to enhance efficiency and global competitiveness.

Sustainability

Prioritizing environmental preservation, renewable energy adoption, and sustainable development practices for long-term growth.

Infrastructure

Developing modern transportation, communication, and urban facilities to support economic and social progress.

Empowerment

Promoting education, skill development, and gender equality to enable individuals and communities to thrive.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Our vision is to build a prosperous, inclusive, and sustainable Bangladesh. Through innovation, resilience, and unity, we are paving the way for a brighter future—one where every citizen can thrive, and our nation stands as a beacon of progress in the global community."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
